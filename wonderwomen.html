Wonder Woman

is an Amazon and was granted her powers by the Greek gods in particular Aphrodite who created the Amazons. It is said that much of her strength comes from her training and channeling her mental powers into physical abilities. Wonder Woman is Princess Diana of the Amazon island Themyscira.

Wonder Woman's Powers

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Superhuman Durability
  • Flight
  • Superhuman Speed
  • Superhuman Reflexes
  • Superhuman Stamina
